UdD ranked 24 in the 2024 World University Rankings for Innovation

Published: June 08, 2024

Universidad de Dagupan proudly achieved Rank 24 in the 2024 World University Rankings for Innovation (WURI) under the Student Support and Engagement category. This accolade highlights the university's exceptional commitment to fostering student growth and involvement. It also emphasizes the innovative support systems and engagement opportunities available to students, showcasing the university's dedication to excellence and student well-being.
