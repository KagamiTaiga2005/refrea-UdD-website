UdD Crowns its New Queen!

Published: April 12, 2024

Introducing the illustrious queens of Universidad de Dagupan's 2024 pageant: Bb. UdD Allyssa Marie Tucay from the School of Business Administration, adorned with grace and poise as she claims the prestigious crown. Alongside her, April Hope Matre of the School of Hospitality shines brightly as the deserving 1st Runner-up, while Angelica Joy Flores, also from the School of Business Administration, captivates as the 2nd Runner-up.


These remarkable women exemplify the diverse talents and outstanding achievements fostered within the halls of UdD, marking a proud moment in the institution's 40-year legacy of excellence and empowerment.

Their triumphs not only showcase their individual brilliance but also mirror the spirit of unity and camaraderie that permeates throughout the UdD community. As ambassadors of the university, these queens embody the values of leadership, resilience, and dedication, inspiring their peers to reach for the stars and pursue their dreams with unwavering determination.


As UdD celebrates four decades of academic excellence and transformative growth, the crowning of these remarkable women serves as a beacon of hope and inspiration for the countless generations of students who will follow in their footsteps.
