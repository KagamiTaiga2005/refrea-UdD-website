UdD Enhances Campus Safety and Drug Prevention Efforts

Published: May 20, 2024

At Universidad de Dagupan, community well-being and safety are paramount. We are committed to creating a healthy university environment by actively engaging in the prevention of illegal drug use. Through various initiatives and programs, we strive to ensure that our campus remains a safe and supportive space for all students.


At UdD, we stand united for a safer community. Our collective efforts reflect our dedication to fostering a secure and nurturing atmosphere where students can thrive. Together, we continue to uphold our commitment to excellence and well-being.
