WHY CHOOSE UNIVERSIDAD de DAGUPAN

At Universidad de Dagupan, excellence isn't just a goal—it's a tradition. With a storied history dating back to 1984, our university has consistently set the standard for academic distinction and holistic development. Our esteemed faculty members are more than just teachers; they're mentors dedicated to guiding you on your journey to success.


What sets Universidad de Dagupan apart is our unwavering focus on real-world engagement. From internships to community service projects, we provide hands-on experiences that prepare you for the challenges of the modern world. Here, learning isn't confined to textbooks—it's a dynamic, immersive experience that equips you with the skills and knowledge you need to thrive in any environment.

40 YEARS OF ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE

KUWAGO CAFE

Kuwago Cafe, nestled in the heart of our university campus, is a beloved sanctuary for students seeking a cozy retreat from their hectic schedules. Adorned with rustic wooden interiors and student artwork, the cafe exudes a warm and inviting ambiance, perfect for both study sessions and social gatherings. Students flock here for the artisanal coffee, especially the signature "Owl’s Brew Latte," alongside an array of delicious pastries and healthy snacks. Its eco-friendly practices and community-centric events, like open mic nights and art exhibits, make Kuwago Cafe a hub of creativity and camaraderie, embodying the vibrant spirit of campus life.

Philosophy

The institution believes that through education, man's God-given gifts are discovered and developed for his personal fulfillment and community uplift.

Vision

It envisions to create a community responsive to the challenges of the changing world.

Mission

It is tasked to prepare the individuals with the best that education can offer in a manner that is consistent with the needs of society.

Objectives

  • To inculcate critical thinking
  • To provide competent human resources in various fields
  • To promote discipline, justice and equality
  • To improve man's quality of life through research and community services
